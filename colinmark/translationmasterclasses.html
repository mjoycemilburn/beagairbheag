Colin Mark English-Gaelic Translation Master Classes

Between 2004 and 2008, Colin Mark, author of the excellent Gaelic-English dictionary, ran a series of of translation classes through the on-line medium of Fòram na Gàidhlig. These provide an entertaining and instructive resource for Gaelic learners.

The posts in the documents listed below were retrieved from the GAIDHLIG-B archive on https://listserv.heanet.ie/cgi-bin/wa. They have been re-formatted to make them easier to read but are otherwise unaltered. Each post is headed by its original title and despatch date.
